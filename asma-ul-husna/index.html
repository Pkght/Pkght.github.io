99 Names of Allah

The beautiful names of Allah (Asma-ul-Husna) with meanings and audio.

Memorization Mode
1

الرَّحْمَنُ

Ar Rahmaan

The Most Merciful

The One who has plenty of mercy for the believers and the blasphemers in this world and especially for the believers in the hereafter.

2

الرَّحِيمُ

Ar Raheem

The Most Compassionate

The One who has plenty of mercy for the believers.

3

الْمَلِكُ

Al Malik

The King

The Sovereign Lord, The One who has absolute ownership and authority over all creation.

4

الْقُدُّوسُ

Al Quddus

The Most Holy

The One who is pure from any imperfection and clear from children and adversaries.

5

السَّلاَمُ

As Salaam

The Source of Peace

The One who grants peace and calm to all creation.

6

الْمُؤْمِنُ

Al Mu'min

The Guardian of Faith

The One who grants safety and security to His creation.

7

الْمُهَيْمِنُ

Al Muhaymin

The Guardian

The One who witnesses the saying and deeds of His creatures.

8

الْعَزِيزُ

Al Azeez

The Almighty

The strong, the defeater who is not defeated.

9

الْجَبَّارُ

Al Jabbaar

The Compeller

The One that nothing happens in His dominion except that which He willed.

10

الْمُتَكَبِّرُ

Al Mutakabbir

The Supreme

The Supreme, The One who is supremely great and above all creation.

11

الْخَالِقُ

Al Khaaliq

The Creator

The One who brings everything from non-existence to existence.

12

الْبَارِئُ

Al Baari

The Maker of Order

The Maker, The Creator who has the power to materialize and proportion His creation.

13

الْمُصَوِّرُ

Al Musawwir

The Shaper of Beauty

The Fashioner, The One who forms His creatures in different shapes and forms.

14

الْغَفَّارُ

Al Ghaffaar

The Forgiving

The One who forgives the sins of His slaves time and time again.

15

الْقَهَّارُ

Al Qahhaar

The Subduer

The Subduer, The One who has perfect Power and absolute control over all things.

16

الْوَهَّابُ

Al Wahhaab

The Bestower

The One who is Generous in giving plenty without any return.

17

الرَّزَّاقُ

Ar Razzaaq

The Provider

The One who provides all the creatures their needs.

18

الْفَتَّاحُ

Al Fattaah

The Opener

The One who opens for His slaves the closed worldly and religious matters.

19

اَلْعَلِيْمُ

Al 'Aleem

The All-Knowing

The One nothing is absent from His knowledge.

20

الْقَابِضُ

Al Qaabid

The Constrictor

The One who constricts the sustenance by His wisdom.

21

الْبَاسِطُ

Al Baasit

The Expander

The One who expands and widens.

22

الْخَافِضُ

Al Khaafid

The Abaser

The One who lowers whoever He willed by His Destruction.

23

الرَّافِعُ

Ar Raafi'

The Exalter

The One who raises whoever He willed by His endowment.

24

الْمُعِزُّ

Al Mu'iz

The Bestower of Honour

He gives esteem to whoever He willed, hence there is no one to degrade Him.

25

المُذِلُّ

Al Mudhil

The Humiliator

The One who degrades whoever he willed, hence there is no one to give him esteem.

26

السَّمِيعُ

As Samee'

The All-Hearing

The One who Hears all things that are heard by His Eternal Hearing without an ear, instrument or organ.

27

الْبَصِيرُ

Al Baseer

The All-Seeing

The One who Sees all things that are seen by His Eternal Seeing without a pupil or any other instrument.

28

الْحَكَمُ

Al Hakam

The Judge

The One who is the judge, the arbitrator, the One who has the complete authority to decide between the creatures.

29

الْعَدْلُ

Al 'Adl

The Just

The One who is entitled to do what He does.

30

اللَّطِيفُ

Al Lateef

The Subtle One

The One who is kind to His slaves and endows upon them.

31

الْخَبِيرُ

Al Khabeer

The All-Aware

The One who knows the truth of things.

32

الْحَلِيمُ

Al Haleem

The Forbearing

The One who delays the punishment for those who deserve it and then He might forgive them.

33

الْعَظِيمُ

Al 'Azeem

The Magnificent

The One deserving the attributes of Exaltment, Glory, Extolement, and complete Majesty.

34

الْغَفُورُ

Al Ghafoor

The Great Forgiver

The One who forgives a lot.

35

الشَّكُورُ

Ash Shakoor

The Rewarder of Thankfulness

The One who gives a lot of reward for a little obedience.

36

الْعَلِيُّ

Al 'Aliyy

The Most High

The One who is clear from the attributes of the creatures.

37

الْكَبِيرُ

Al Kabeer

The Most Great

The One who is greater than everything in status.

38

الْحَفِيظُ

Al Hafeez

The Preserver

The One who protects whatever and whoever He willed to protect.

39

المُقيِت

Al Muqeet

The Nourisher

The One who has the Power.

40

الْحسِيبُ

Al Haseeb

The Reckoner

The One who gives the satisfaction.

41

الْجَلِيلُ

Al Jaleel

The Majestic

The One who is attributed with greatness of Power and Glory of status.

42

الْكَرِيمُ

Al Kareem

The Generous

The One who is clear from abjectness.

43

الرَّقِيبُ

Ar Raqeeb

The Watchful One

The One that nothing is absent from Him.

44

الْمُجِيبُ

Al Mujeeb

The Responder to Prayer

The One who answers the one in need if he asks Him and rescues the yearner if he calls upon Him.

45

الْوَاسِعُ

Al Waasi'

The All-Encompassing

The Knowledgeable; The One nothing is absent from His knowledge.

46

الْحَكِيمُ

Al Hakeem

The Perfectly Wise

The One who is correct in His doings.

47

الْوَدُودُ

Al Wudood

The Loving One

The One who loves His believing slaves and His believing slaves love Him.

48

الْمَجِيدُ

Al Majeed

The Most Glorious One

The One who is with perfect Power, High Status, Compassion, Generosity and Kindness.

49

الْبَاعِثُ

Al Baa'ith

The Resurrector

The One who resurrects His slaves after death for reward and/or punishment.

50

الشَّهِيدُ

Ash Shaheed

The Witness

The Witness, The One from whom nothing is hidden.

51

الْحَقُّ

Al Haqq

The Truth

The One who truly exists.

52

الْوَكِيلُ

Al Wakeel

The Trustee

The Trustee, The One who is relied upon for all affairs.

53

الْقَوِيُّ

Al Qawiyy

The Possessor of all strength

The One with the complete Power.

54

الْمَتِينُ

Al Mateen

The Forceful

The Firm, The One with steadfast power who never tires.

55

الْوَلِيُّ

Al Waliyy

The Protector

The Protecting Friend, The One who supports and manages the affairs of His servants.

56

الْحَمِيدُ

Al Hameed

The Praiseworthy

The praised One who deserves to be praised.

57

الْمُحْصِي

Al Muhsi

The Reckoner

The One who the count of things are known to him.

58

الْمُبْدِئُ

Al Mubdi

The Originator

The One who started the human being. That is, He created him.

59

الْمُعِيدُ

Al Mu'eed

The Restorer

The One who brings back the creatures after death.

60

الْمُحْيِي

Al Muhiy

The Giver of Life

The One who took out a living human from semen that does not have a soul.

61

اَلْمُمِيتُ

Al Mumeet

The Bringer of Death

The One who renders the living dead.

62

الْحَيُّ

Al Haiyy

The Ever-Living

The One attributed with a life that is neither born nor is it coming to an end.

63

الْقَيُّومُ

Al Qayyoom

The Self-Sustaining

The One who remains and does not end.

64

الْوَاجِدُ

Al Waajid

The Finder

The Rich who is never poor. Al-Wajd is Richness.

65

الْمَاجِدُ

Al Maajid

The Most Glorious

The One who is Majid.

66

الْواحِدُ

Al Waahid

The Only One

The One without a partner.

67

اَلاَحَدُ

Al Ahad

The Unique

The One who is unique in His essence and attributes.

68

الصَّمَدُ

As Samad

The Eternal

The Master who is relied upon in matters and reverted to in ones needs.

69

الْقَادِرُ

Al Qaadir

The Capable

The One attributed with Power.

70

الْمُقْتَدِرُ

Al Muqtadir

The Creator of all power

The One with the perfect Power that nothing is withheld from Him.

71

الْمُقَدِّمُ

Al Muqaddim

The Expediter

The One who puts things in their right place. He makes ahead what He wills.

72

الْمُؤَخِّرُ

Al Mu'akhir

The Delayer

The One who puts things in their right place. He makes behind what He wills.

73

الأوَّلُ

Al Awwal

The First

The One whose Existence is without a beginning.

74

الآخِرُ

Al Aakhir

The Last

The One whose Existence is without an end.

75

الظَّاهِرُ

Az Zaahir

The Manifest

The One that nothing is above Him and nothing is underneath Him, hence He exists without a place.

76

الْبَاطِنُ

Al Baatin

The Hidden

The One that nothing is above Him and nothing is underneath Him, hence He exists without a place.

77

الْوَالِي

Al Waali

The Governor

The One who owns things and manages them.

78

الْمُتَعَالِي

Al Muta'ali

The Self Exalted

The One who is clear from the attributes of the creation.

79

الْبَرُّ

Al Barr

The Source of All Goodness

The One who is kind to His creatures, who covered them with His sustenance and specified whoever He willed among them by His support, protection, and special mercy.

80

التَّوَابُ

At Tawwaab

The Acceptor of Repentance

The One who grants repentance to whoever He willed among His creatures and accepts his repentance.

81

الْمُنْتَقِمُ

Al Muntaqim

The Avenger

The One who victoriously prevails over His enemies and punishes them for their sins.

82

العَفُوُّ

Al Afuww

The Pardoner

The One with wide forgiveness.

83

الرَّؤُوفُ

Ar Ra'oof

The Most Kind

The One with extreme Mercy. The Mercy of Allah is His will to endow upon whoever He willed among His creatures.

84

مَالِكُ الْمُلْكِ

Maalik Ul Mulk

Master of the Kingdom

The One who controls the Dominion and gives dominion to whoever He willed.

85

ذُوالْجَلاَلِ وَالإكْرَامِ

Dhu Al Jalaali Wa Al Ikraam

Lord of Majesty and Bounty

The One who deserves to be Exalted and not denied.

86

الْمُقْسِطُ

Al Muqsit

The Equitable

The One who is Just in His judgment.

87

الْجَامِعُ

Al Jaami'

The Gatherer

The One who gathers the creatures on a day that there is no doubt about, that is the Day of Judgment.

88

الْغَنِيُّ

Al Ghaniyy

The Self-Sufficient

The One who does not need the creation.

89

الْمُغْنِي

Al Mughi

The Enricher

The One who satisfies the necessities of the creatures.

90

اَلْمَانِعُ

Al Maani'

The Preventer of harm

The Supporter who protects and gives victory to His pious believers.

91

الضَّارَّ

Ad Daaarr

The Harmer

The One who makes harm reach to whoever He willed and benefit to whoever He willed.

92

النَّافِعُ

An Naafi'

The Benefactor

The One who gives benefit to whoever He willed.

93

النُّورُ

An Noor

The Light

The One who guides.

94

الْهَادِي

Al Haadi

The Guider

The One who guides.

95

الْبَدِيعُ

Al Badi'

The Incomparable Originator

The One who created the creation and formed it without any preceding example.

96

اَلْبَاقِي

Al Baaqi

The Everlasting One

The One that the state of non-existence is impossible for Him.

97

الْوَارِثُ

Al Waarith

The Inheritor

The One whose Existence remains.

98

الرَّشِيدُ

Ar Rasheed

The Most Righteous Guide

The One who guides.

99

الصَّبُورُ

As Saboor

The Patient One

The One who does not quickly punish the sinners.
