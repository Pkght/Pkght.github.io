Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Information We Collect

Client-Side Processing Only

The Islamic tools on this website are designed with privacy as a core principle:

No Data Collection

  • We do not collect, store, or share any personal information or financial data you enter
  • All calculations are performed within your browser (client-side)
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or third parties

Third-Party API Data

When using the Nisab Values page with live data:

  • • We fetch gold/silver prices from IslamicAPI for display purposes only

No Personal Data Usage

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use your information in any way. Your financial calculations remain completely private on your device.

All calculations happen in your browser. No sensitive data leaves your device.

No Data Storage

We don't store your information, so there's no risk of data breaches from our servers.
