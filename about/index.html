About QuickNisab

Empowering the global Muslim community with accurate, privacy-focused, and easy-to-use Islamic calculators.

Our Mission

Fulfilling religious obligations like Zakat and Inheritance distribution can be complex. Mathematical precision is required alongside adherence to Islamic rulings (Fiqh).

QuickNisab was built to bridge this gap. Our mission is to provide accessible, accurate, and trustworthy tools that help Muslims perform their duties with confidence. We believe that technology should simplify these processes without compromising on privacy or religious authenticity.

Methodology & Sources

Authentic Sources

Our calculators adhere to standard Sunni jurisprudence (Fiqh). We consult authoritative texts and scholarly consensuses ensuring that calculations for Zakat and Inheritance meet accepted Islamic criteria.

Real-Time Accuracy

We utilize reliable third-party APIs (such as IslamicAPI) to fetch real-time market data for Gold and Silver prices, ensuring your Zakat liability is calculated based on current value.

Our Core Values

Privacy First

Transparency

Community Focused
