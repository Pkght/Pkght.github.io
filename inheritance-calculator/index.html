Understanding Islamic Inheritance (Mirath / Faraid)

Islamic inheritance is a structured system defined in the Quran and Sunnah. It ensures fairness among heirs, protects the rights of family members, and prevents disputes. This calculator estimates fixed shares for primary heirs according to widely accepted rules.

Primary Heirs & Shares

  • Husband: 1/2 if no children, 1/4 if children exist
  • Wife: 1/4 if no children, 1/8 if children exist
  • Son: receives twice the share of a daughter
  • Daughter: single daughter 1/2, two or more share 2/3
  • Father: 1/6 if children exist, otherwise may take residuary
  • Mother: 1/6 if children exist, 1/3 if no children

Other relatives may inherit if primary heirs are absent. This tool covers common cases for practical use.
