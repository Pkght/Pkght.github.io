Nisab Values

Nisab is the minimum wealth threshold a Muslim must reach before Zakat becomes obligatory. Below are the current standard quantities based on Islamic rulings.

Gold & Silver Thresholds

Type Quantity Description
Gold 87.48 grams Gold nisab threshold for Zakat obligation
Silver 612.36 grams Silver nisab threshold; widely used to help more people

How to Use Nisab Values

1. Multiply the nisab quantity by the current market price per gram in your local currency.
2. Compare this total to your net wealth (total assets minus debts).
3. If your net wealth is equal to or exceeds the nisab, Zakat becomes obligatory at 2.5%.

For accurate calculations, use the Zakat Calculator, which computes your net wealth and Zakat automatically.
